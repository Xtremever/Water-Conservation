What is Water Conservation?

Shower for fewer minutes

While you are brushing your teeth, turn off the water.

While you are shaving, turn off the water.

Install flow restrictors or water-saving shower-heads.

Avoid leaving the water running when rinsing dishes when washing them by hand.

Check for leaks in the pipes and faucets.

Just when your yard needs it, water it.

When washing your car, avoid using the hose.

Tell your kids not to play with the sprinklers/hose.
