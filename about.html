What is Water Conservation?

The method of conserving water involves using it wisely to cut down on wasteful water use.

Importance

Over the past 50 years, the U.S. population has doubled while our desire for water has tripled. Water conservation is essential since at least 40 states expect to experience water shortages by 2024. “Water conservation is crucial”, says Fresh Water Watch, because clean, fresh water is a scarce and expensive resource.
