What Happens If Not Saving Water?

Earth's pure freshwater supplies are extremely scarce and are depleting annually as a result of contamination from causes like garbage, leaks, industrial waste, untreated sewage, ineffective irrigation systems, and oil spills. Some regions of the world see rain for nearly the entire year. Major issues still exist for a number of reasons, including drainage, storage, evaporation, and hygiene. The majority of people still use water carelessly and are unaware of the importance of water conservation. The cost of building infrastructure to supply water to homes and businesses is rising to an unaffordable level.
